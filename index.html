About Tykoon Wood Floors

Founded in 2005 by master craftsman Michael Tykoon, our company began with a simple mission: to provide Los Angeles homeowners with the highest quality hardwood floor refinishing services. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the most trusted names in floor restoration, serving thousands of satisfied customers across the greater LA area.

Our core values of craftsmanship, integrity, and customer satisfaction drive everything we do. We believe in using only premium materials, employing expert technicians, and standing behind our work with industry-leading guarantees. Our commitment to excellence has earned us countless five-star reviews and a reputation as Los Angeles' premier hardwood floor refinishing service.
